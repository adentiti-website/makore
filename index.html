Why Makore

What the game is built to teach.

See threats through stories.

Scenario cards are inspired by real events from small business breaches to everyday social engineering. You won’t just memorise terms; you’ll remember the stories.

Practice decisions that matter.

Each turn asks: ignore, escalate, patch, educate, or invest? Makore rewards long-term thinking, not lucky dice rolls.

Build habits, not just scores.

“Awareness” and “Resilience” tracks nudge players toward daily cyber hygiene—strong passwords, backups, updates, and team communication.
