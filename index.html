Meet the Threats

Cyber threats are no longer invisible. In MAKORE, they take form so you can recognize them, understand them, and defeat them.

Whisper

Cyberbullying that spreads harm through messages, posts, and pressure.

Echo

Phishing & insider threats that imitate trust to steal sensitive information.

ShadowCode

Malware & zero-day attacks that silently infiltrate systems.

LockJaw

Ransomware attacks that lock your systems and demand payment.

Flood

Spam overload that hides dangerous links within endless noise.

Blackout

Cloud outages that disrupt access to critical systems and data.
